Biology in Everyday Life:
Diagnosing Diabetes lab

Expaination:

This artifact is a packet of information pertaining to the endocrine system. It was used as background to a topic we covered that involved feedback loops, and homeostasis. It contains information on the hypothalymus and the major hormones the pituitary gland produces. This page also gives information on what each hormone does. It talks about how the endocrine system regulates the body using feedback loops and the hormones.

This artifact relates to everyday life because your body is constanty doing this even when you don't realize it. The endocrine system regulates body temperature, glucose intake and water retention. This artifact shows how important the system is, and without it, we could not survive.
