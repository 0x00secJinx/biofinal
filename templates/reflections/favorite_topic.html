Favorite Topic:
BioTech

Explaination:

This artifact shows my favorite topic from this course, biotech. It shows many cool new methods of genetically modifying organisms and explains how they work. This artifact also gives examples of where this field of research is useful, such as forensics, gene therapy and GMO's.

This is my favorite topic because of how new and recent this research is. Also, this field is changing how we can live our lives. For example, using GMO's we now can guaruntee a full harvest every year which puts food on the table. We can also use it to help make specialized medications for people based on their bodily needs. Another way they can use this is to make proteins that the body needs from bacteria and then insert the proteins into the body, for example, insulin. Insulin that is neccessary to make ATP can be made with biotech to help people with diabetes.
