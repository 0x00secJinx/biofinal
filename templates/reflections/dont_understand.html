A Topic I still do not fully understand:
Plant Behaviors

Expaination:

This artifact is a study sheet explaining many of plants behaviors. It shows the stimuli and the plant's response. It also gives information on defenses such as thorns for herbivores and the waxy layer outside to keep pathogens out. This artifact was used as a study guide for a quiz that I bombed because I did not look over this when I should have.

The reason I chose this as topic I don't understand is because there is a lot of memorization that goes along with it. We had to remember all the names and what kind of stimuli and the reaction of the plant. This was a lot of information to take in, and as stated previously, I did not look at it when I was supposed to for the quiz and then never looked at it again.
