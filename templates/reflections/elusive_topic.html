The "Elusive" Topic:
Water Potential

Explaination:

Water potential is my "elusive" topic. I can not understand it to this day still. Water potential is the net movement of water through a gradient. Water travels from high water potential to low water potential. This artifact is a water potential worksheet we had for homework. I tried my best, but I got most of them wrong.

The way that water potential is calculated is what makes it hard for me to get. All the water potentials are negative with the exception of a few with pressure accounted for. The lower the number, the lower the water potential. The calculations are not what gives me trouble, but figuring out which way the water travels is the tricky part.
