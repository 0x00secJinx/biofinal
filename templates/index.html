Introduction

My name is Mason and this e-portfolio is for an AP Biology Final Project. Artifacts on this e-portfolio come from my experiences in this class. This website also provides the record of my final experiment I did for this class. The experiment was a bioinformatics experiment that looked at the H and N proteins on different strains of the flu virus and the proteins of the strains in the vaccines. The reflection section of this website gives my view on overviewing questions and debates in the field of biology.
